01 INTRODUCTION
 
About 
KDI POWER 
KDI Power Private Limited is an esteemed manufacturer of wires and cables in India, 
committed to delivering reliable electrical solutions across industries. An ISO 9001-certified 
organisation, the company manufactures a wide range of products including Power Cables, 
Control Cables, Aerial Bunched (ABC) Cables, House Wires and Cables, and has emerged as a 
trusted, DISCOM-approved name in the Indian cable industry. 
Founded in 2018 by Mr. Sahil Bansal, Managing Director, KDI Power operates a modern 
manufacturing facility at the DSIDC Industrial Area, Narela, Delhi. The company serves sectors 
including power generation and transmission, oil and gas, metro and railways, construction, 
telecommunications and shipping, producing a comprehensive range of Conductors, XLPE 
Cables, Aerial Bunched Cables, Solar Cables, Submersible Cables, Rubber Cables and PVC 
cables with FRLS, ZHFR and TPR variants, covering all categories of LT cables. 
With a clear vision for growth, KDI Power has undertaken an ambitious expansion programme 
to manufacture the complete range of ACSR Conductors, LT Power & Control Cables, Aerial 
Bunched Cables, Solar Cables, Submersible Cables, Instrumentation Cables and Rubber 
Cables. 
 
Our Commitment to Quality 
KDI Power’s Quality Policy is driven by a company-wide commitment to excellence. Every employee 
contributes to our mission of meeting and exceeding national and international standards to ensure complete 
customer satisfaction.

Aerial Bunched Cables (ABC) are used for overhead power distribution in cities where ground space is constrained. These cables use several insulated phase conductors bundled tightly together, usually with a bare neutral conductor.
This system is ideal for rural distribution and especially attractive for installation in difficult terrains such as hilly, forest & coastal areas etc.
Insulation

Voltage Grade

Screening
Screened by an extruded semi conducting layer – XLPE Insulated.
Screened again by an extruded semi conducting layer and a copper tape.
Applications
Low voltage ABC are developed as they promise to be cheaper, safer, more reliable, require less tree clearing and pruning, be more aesthetic, be less labor intensive, require less maintenance and eliminate bushfires being initiated by conductor clashing.
Advantages
Relative immunity to short circuits caused by external forces (wind, fallen branches), unless they abrade the insulation.
Can stand in close proximity to trees/buildings and will not generate sparks if touched.
Simpler installation, as crossbars and insulators are not required.
