Features These wires are manufactured using bright-annealed, 99.97% pure bare copper conductors, ensuring low conductor resistance and efficient electrical performance. The sheathing is made with a specially formulated PVC compound that allows easy stripping while providing strong resistance to mechanical abrasion during use. The PVC compounds used for insulation and sheathing feature a high oxygen and temperature index, helping to limit the spread of fire and enhance overall safety.
