Compared to conventional bare conductor overhead systems, Aerial Bunched Cables offer a more advanced and reliable solution for overhead power distribution. They provide enhanced safety, improved reliability, reduced power losses, and greater system efficiency by lowering installation, maintenance, and operational costs. This system is particularly suitable for rural electrification and is highly effective in challenging terrains such as hilly regions, forest areas, and coastal locations. Aerial Bunched Cables are also an excellent choice for power distribution in congested urban areas with narrow streets and lanes. In rapidly developing urban environments, ABC systems provide the flexibility required for easy rerouting to accommodate changes in urban infrastructure and development plans. Construction Of Cable KDI Power Aerial Bundled Cables (ABC) are designed with phase conductors that can be configured for single-phase or three-phase systems. A lighting conductor can also be incorporated to support street lighting applications. The messenger conductor carries the mechanical load of the cable and maintains the assembly under proper tension. The phase conductors are manufactured from concentrically stranded aluminium wires consisting of 7 or 19 strands, while the messenger conductor is made from aluminium alloy with 7 or 19 strands for enhanced strength. Phase and lighting conductors are insulated with XLPE compound using advanced Sioplas technology , ensuring durability and reliable performance. The messenger conductor may be either bare or insulated depending on the application. During assembly, the phase and lighting conductors are twisted around the messenger conductor. In certain applications, a separate neutral conductor and street lighting cable can also be provided. Typically, the messenger conductor functions as both the neutral and the earthing conductor in the system. Design Configuration Single Phase or Three Phase systems with or without Neutral Conductor and Street Light line. Conductor Phase or Lighting conductor: Electrical grade Aluminium of H2 or H4 grade as per IS: 8130 Messenger conductor: Aluminium Magnesium and Silica Alloy confirming to IS:398 (Part – 4) Insulation XLPE with Sioplas Technique.
