LT Control Cables
(Low Tension Control Cables) are specialized flexible cables used to transmit mechanical force or motion between two points, typically for controlling machinery, equipment, or vehicles. They are designed to provide precise and smooth operation with minimal effort and are widely used in applications such as elevators, cranes, automotive systems, marine equipment, and industrial machinery.
