These wires can operate at temperatures up to 85°C as they are insulated with a high-quality flame-retardant PVC compound formulated and manufactured in-house. Manufactured using minimum 99.97% electrolytic grade annealed bare copper with more than 100% conductivity , the wires ensure efficient power transmission. Suitable for installation in lighting fittings and electrical appliances up to 1100 V (AC) , these wires perform reliably even under adverse atmospheric conditions.
