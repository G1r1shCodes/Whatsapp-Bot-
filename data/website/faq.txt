KDI Power is an LT cable and wire manufacturer based in Narela, Delhi. Our range covers XLPE and PVC armoured LT power cables (IS:7098), control cables (IS:1554), aerial bunched cables / ABC (IS:14255), submersible pump cables (IS:694 / IS:9968), DC solar cables, FR/FRLS house wires and building cables (IS:694), flexible industrial cables, rubber-insulated cables, marine and offshore cables, and instrumentation/screened cables. All products are manufactured to relevant Indian Standards for power distribution, industrial, rural electrification, and building applications. Where is KDI Power's manufacturing facility located? Our manufacturing unit is located at 1243, Block H, DSIDC Industrial Area, Narela, New Delhi – 110040, India. Narela is a major industrial estate in north Delhi, giving us reliable supply and dispatch access across Delhi-NCR and to DISCOM and EPC projects across India. Is KDI Power a quality-certified manufacturer? Yes. KDI Power operates an ISO 9001 certified quality management system, and our products are manufactured in compliance with the applicable Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS / IS) specifications for each cable type. We are also a GeM-registered vendor, allowing government departments and PSUs to procure from us directly through the Government e-Marketplace. Which IS standards do KDI Power cables comply with? Our cables are built to the relevant Indian Standards for each product: IS:7098 (XLPE power cables), IS:1554 (PVC-insulated control and power cables), IS:14255 (aerial bunched cables), IS:694 (PVC house wires and flexible cables), and IS:9968 (submersible and flexible cables). Quoting the correct IS standard on your enquiry helps us supply the exact specification your project requires. How do I request a quote or place an order? You can reach KDI Power by phone at +91 85959 40069 or email info@kdipower.com, or submit an enquiry through the contact form on our website. Please share the cable type, IS standard, conductor size/cores, voltage grade, and quantity so we can give you an accurate quotation quickly. Does KDI Power export cables and wires internationally? Yes. KDI Power manufactures LT cables and wires for export alongside our domestic supply to Indian DISCOMs and EPC contractors.
