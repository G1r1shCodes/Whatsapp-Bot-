Option A: Marketing Executive / Assistant Manager (B2B & Channel Marketing) Job Title: Marketing Executive / Assistant Manager (B2B & Channel Marketing) Location: Narela, New Delhi (DSIDC Industrial Area) Job Type: Full-Time Department: Sales & Marketing Experience: 2–5 years in B2B marketing, building material, or electrical/cable industry (Preferred) About the Role: KDI Power is seeking a dynamic, result-oriented Marketing Executive / Assistant Manager to join our growing team. In this role, you will be responsible for driving brand awareness, expanding our dealer/distributor network, and executing offline and online marketing campaigns to position KDI Power as the preferred choice for wires and cables in residential, commercial, and industrial markets. Key Responsibilities: Channel & Dealer Marketing: Develop and maintain strong marketing relationships with distributors, dealers, electrical contractors, and builders. Lead Generation: Execute promotional strategies to generate quality leads for our sales team across various product categories (Power, Solar, House, and Control Cables). Brand Management: Manage production and distribution of point-of-sale (POS) materials, catalogs, brochures, and promotional merchandise. Market Research & Intelligence: Analyze competitor products, pricing structures, and industry trends to identify new growth opportunities. Event & Exhibition Management: Coordinate and participate in trade shows, local dealer meets, and industry exhibitions to showcase KDI Power’s range of products. Coordination: Work closely with the Sales and Product teams to align marketing campaigns with business objectives. Requirements & Qualifications: Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Marketing, or a related field (MBA is a plus). 2+ years of experience in offline marketing or channel partner management, ideally within the electrical, wires/cables, construction materials, or manufacturing sector. Excellent communication, negotiation, and interpersonal skills. Willingness to travel locally for market visits and dealer engagement sessions. Highly organized, proactive, and self-motivated.
