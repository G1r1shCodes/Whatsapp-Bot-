True to its name and inherited values, KDI Power Private Limited has begun to display such promise, steadily establishing itself as a trusted name in the cable manufacturing industry. Founded in 2018 by Mr. Sahil Bansal (Managing Director), KDI Power Private Limited has built a modern manufacturing facility at the DSIIDC Industrial Area, Narela, Delhi – 110040 to meet the growing demand for high-quality cables. Serving sectors such as electrical appliances, electronics, power generation, transmission, mining, and shipping, the company produces a wide range of cables Elastomeric cables, XLPE cables, AB Cable, PVC cables with FRLS,ZHFR, TPR, and All Kind of LT Cables. Beyond standard products, KDI Power specializes in custom-designed cables tailored to meet specific customer requirements and industry specifications. Backed by a team of qualified engineers, skilled technicians, and dedicated professionals, the company enforces strict quality control at every stage of production. With a vision for growth, KDI Power has embarked on an ambitious expansion program to manufacture the complete range of LT Power & Control Cables, AB Cables, Instrumentation Cables, and Rubber Cables.
